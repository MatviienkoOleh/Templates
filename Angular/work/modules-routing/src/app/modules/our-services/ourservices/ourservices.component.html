FSMA Compliance

To help clients meet FDA regulations, Northstar offers access to FSMA compliant outlets for both packaged and unpacked organic waste. Additionally, our team has Food Safety Preventative Controls Alliance (FSPCA) certified staff to assist with new FSMA regulations and requirements.

Quality Control

At Northstar, culture and integrity are essential parts of doing business. Therefore, we work with clients to provide disposal solutions that maintain the integrity of their brand. This includes offering certificates of destruction for packaged or unpackaged materials, as well as conducting outlet site audits to ensure continued quality service. Multiple Northstar team members are also certified quality auditors (QMS ISO9001 lead auditor), capable of performing quality audits on any outlets.
